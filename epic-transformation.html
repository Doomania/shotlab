Common mistakes

  • Using slow pacing — anime battle energy requires fast or frantic.
  • Missing style direction — specify anime subgenre.
  • Choosing too many moves — 2–3 maximum for a 4-second clip.

FAQ

Best camera moves for anime battle?+
Fast Dolly In, Side Tracking, and Whip Pan are the core trio. Add Dutch Angle for villain moments and Orbit for hero power reveals.
9:16 or 16:9?+
16:9 for cinematic scale. 9:16 for Douyin/TikTok distribution.
Best style description?+
'High-energy shonen anime with bold speed lines' covers most cases. For darker content: 'seinen dark anime, high contrast, desaturated.'
