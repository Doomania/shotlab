About Luma prompt formatting

Luma Dream Machine responds exceptionally well to smooth camera movements and atmospheric, mood-driven prose. Include how the scene feels, not just what it looks like.

Default ratio: 16:9

Tips for Luma prompts

  • Include mood and atmosphere in your description.
  • Write camera movement naturally mid-sentence: 'as the camera drifts forward...'
  • Luma handles smooth motion exceptionally — great for Steadicam and dolly work.
  • Specify emotional tone: 'dreamlike', 'ethereal', 'grounded', 'melancholy'.
  • 16:9 is the primary supported ratio.

FAQ

What prompt style works for Luma?+
Atmospheric, mood-driven prose. Describe how the scene feels, not just what it shows.
Is Luma good for smooth camera moves?+
Yes — one of the best for smooth, fluid motion. Steadicam, Dolly, and Orbit shots look polished.
What aspect ratios does Luma support?+
Primarily 16:9. Check Luma's current documentation for latest supported ratios.
Is Luma good for anime?+
Luma tends more toward photorealistic and cinematic. Be very explicit about anime style if that is your goal.
