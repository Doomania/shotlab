About Shot Lab

Shot Lab is a free, browser-based AI video prompt utility for creators who work with AI video generation tools.

Shot Lab helps you describe camera movement clearly — the part of an AI video prompt most creators get wrong. Choose from 38 camera moves, each with a looping animated demo and copy-ready prompt phrase.

What Shot Lab does

  • Visual reference for 38 professional camera movements.
  • Model-ready prompts for Seedance, Kling, Runway, Veo, Pika, and Luma.
  • Starter templates: anime action, music video, product reveal, and more.
  • Advanced 3D Camera Studio to physically block a move and export a prompt.
  • Runs entirely in the browser. No sign-in. No account. No server.
