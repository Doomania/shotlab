Common mistakes

  • Using slow pacing — transformation shots should be fast to explosive.
  • Missing energy description — specify aura colour, particle type, shockwave direction.
  • Only using one camera move — transformation sequences earn 2–3 compound moves.

FAQ

Best move for a transformation?+
Orbit + Crane Up is the classic combination. Add a Fast Dolly In at the peak moment for maximum impact.
What style works?+
'Epic shonen anime transformation style' for the big powerup. Specify aura colour: 'golden energy', 'dark purple power', 'ice-blue crystalline.'
How to make it feel big?+
Volumetric god rays, expanding shockwave, energy particles, intense rim backlighting. Crane Up reveals scale.
