What is a Drone Dive (FPV)?

An aggressive aerial movement where a racing drone descends rapidly toward a target. The FPV camera captures the visceral, rushing perspective of a bird of prey.

When to use it

Use for high-adrenaline openings, action punctuation, or any moment needing raw aerial speed.

Emotional effect

Visceral speed, adrenaline, threat, arrival.

Prompt formula

Base phrase: an aggressive FPV drone dive toward the target

Full formula: [Subject] + Camera: an aggressive FPV drone dive toward the target + [Style] + [Lighting] + [Pacing] + [Ratio]

Common mistakes

  • Confusing with a regular aerial shot — specify the aggression and speed explicitly.
  • Using slow pacing — the movement requires fast or frantic pacing.
  • Not specifying the target — state what the camera is diving toward.

FAQ

What is FPV?+
First Person View — a small racing drone with a camera giving a highly dynamic, fluid first-person perspective.
Veo format?+
'The camera performs an aggressive FPV-style drone dive plunging from altitude toward the target at high speed.'
Drone Dive vs Crane Down?+
Crane Down is a controlled mechanical descent — smooth and deliberate. Drone Dive is aggressive and fast, rushing toward the subject like a hawk.
