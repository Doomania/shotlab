Common mistakes

  • Using smooth or Steadicam — war content needs handheld shake for authenticity.
  • Under-describing the environment — burned vehicles, rubble, smoke, dust are essential.
  • Using slow pacing — war action demands fast or frantic.

FAQ

Why does handheld make war content feel real?+
It replicates the physical experience of moving under stress. News footage and war documentaries use handheld — audiences associate the shake with authenticity.
Best moves for war scenes?+
Handheld is the foundation. POV Walk for immersion. Fast Dolly In for impact moments. Whip Pan for chaotic transitions.
Best AI model for war content?+
Runway Gen-3 and Kling handle gritty live-action well. Veo produces strong photorealistic results. Always specify 'documentary style' or 'war cinema style.'
