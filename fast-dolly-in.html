Common mistakes

  • Using handheld for product shots — shake reads as low budget.
  • Missing lighting detail — product shots live and die by the light.
  • Choosing too many moves — orbit + one push is usually the maximum.

FAQ

Best moves for product reveal?+
Orbit Shot and Slow Dolly In/Macro Push. Pedestal Up for dramatic packaging reveals.
Best aspect ratio?+
1:1 for Instagram feed, 9:16 for Stories/Reels, 16:9 for YouTube.
Best lighting for premium products?+
'Soft diffused key light, crisp rim on a dark background.' Add a colour to the rim: 'amber rim', 'cool blue rim.'
