Common mistakes

  • Not specifying neon colours — pink+cyan, purple+green, blue+orange create completely different aesthetics.
  • Using a static shot — cyberpunk energy requires movement.
  • Forgetting rain and reflective surfaces — essential to the cyberpunk visual language.

FAQ

What makes a prompt feel truly cyberpunk?+
Specific neon colours, rain or wet surfaces for reflections, and a busy urban environment. Add 'volumetric mist' for depth.
Best moves for cyberpunk street?+
Steadicam follow and POV Walk for street-level immersion. Drone Fly-Over for establishing city scale.
Best aspect ratio?+
9:16 for mobile platforms — vertical frame emphasises neon-lit architecture.
