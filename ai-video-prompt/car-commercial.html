Common mistakes

  • Using slow pacing — steady to fast is expected in automotive.
  • Choosing static or handheld shots — automotive needs fluid, dynamic movement.
  • Not specifying the car colour, type, and environment.

FAQ

Best moves for car commercial?+
Drone Fly-Over, Low-Angle Side Tracking, and Fast Dolly In. Add FPV Drone Dive for aggressive energy.
Best lighting?+
Golden-hour sun for warm rim light. Wet streets for neon reflections at night. Overcast for even moody commercial light.
Best aspect ratio?+
16:9 for YouTube and widescreen. 9:16 for TikTok/Instagram.
