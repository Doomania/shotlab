Common mistakes

  • Using fast pacing — landscape reveals require slow, majestic pacing.
  • Not specifying time of day — golden hour, blue hour, overcast all produce dramatically different light.
  • No sense of scale — include a small human, structure, or reference point.

FAQ

Best time of day for landscape?+
Golden hour (hour after sunrise / before sunset) for cinematic warmth and long shadows. Blue hour (just after sunset) for moody cool tones.
Best AI model for landscape reveals?+
Veo and Runway handle landscape well. Luma excels at atmospheric, dreamlike landscape footage.
How to make a landscape feel vast?+
Include a scale reference — a person, a road, a building that appears tiny. Use Crane Down rather than just fly-over to descend into the scene.
