Camera Movement Glossary for AI Video Prompts

Definitions, emotional effects, and copy-ready prompt phrases for every major camera movement used in AI video generation.

Dolly In

Prompt phrase: a slow dolly push-in

What: Physically moves camera forward — changes perspective.  |  Effect: Tension, focus, intimacy.

Dolly Out

Prompt phrase: a slow dolly pull-back

What: Camera glides away from subject.  |  Effect: Isolation, release, scale.

Fast Dolly In

Prompt phrase: a fast dolly rush-in

What: Rapid charge toward the subject.  |  Effect: Shock, urgency, impact.

Vertigo Dolly Zoom

Prompt phrase: a Vertigo dolly-zoom

What: Camera back + zoom in simultaneously.  |  Effect: Disorientation, dread.

Orbit Shot

Prompt phrase: a 180-degree orbit

What: Camera circles the subject.  |  Effect: Hero energy, dimensionality.

Crane Up

Prompt phrase: a crane rise

What: Camera ascends on a mechanical arm.  |  Effect: Grandeur, finality, scale.

Crane Down

Prompt phrase: a crane descent

What: Camera descends from above.  |  Effect: Arrival, intimacy after scale.

Whip Pan

Prompt phrase: a fast whip-pan with motion blur

What: Pan so fast the frame blurs.  |  Effect: Speed, kinetic energy.

Handheld

Prompt phrase: raw handheld with natural shake

What: Operator-held, organic wobble.  |  Effect: Urgency, authenticity.

Rack Focus

Prompt phrase: a rack focus between planes

What: Shifts focus through depth.  |  Effect: Redirects attention.

Dutch Angle

Prompt phrase: a canted Dutch-angle roll

What: Camera rotated on lens axis.  |  Effect: Unease, wrongness.

Drone Fly-Over

Prompt phrase: a high drone fly-over

What: Aerial sweep from altitude.  |  Effect: Geography, scale.

Drone Dive FPV

Prompt phrase: an aggressive FPV drone dive

What: Fast downward aerial plunge.  |  Effect: Adrenaline, arrival.

POV Walk

Prompt phrase: a first-person POV walk

What: Eye-height with head-bob.  |  Effect: Immersion, embodiment.

Static Shot

Prompt phrase: a locked-off static frame

What: No camera movement.  |  Effect: Stillness, observation.

Steadicam Glide

Prompt phrase: a smooth Steadicam glide

What: Smooth weightless following.  |  Effect: Dreamlike, immersive.

Over the Shoulder

Prompt phrase: over-the-shoulder framing

What: Scene framed past foreground figure.  |  Effect: Grounds dialogue.

Side Tracking

Prompt phrase: a lateral side-tracking shot

What: Camera moves alongside subject.  |  Effect: Parallax depth.

Fisheye

Prompt phrase: a fisheye lens

What: Extreme wide-angle, bends lines.  |  Effect: Distortion, surreal.

Leading Shot

Prompt phrase: a leading shot backward

What: Camera retreats as subject approaches.  |  Effect: Momentum, connection.

Following Shot

Prompt phrase: a following shot trailing behind

What: Camera stays behind subject.  |  Effect: Journey, immersion.

FAQ

Dolly vs zoom?+
Dolly moves the camera — perspective changes, background grows. Zoom changes focal length — image compresses flat.
How many moves in one prompt?+
1–3. Use 'then' to chain them.
Which move for anime action?+
Fast Dolly In + Side Tracking + Whip Pan.
